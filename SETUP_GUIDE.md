## First Time Setup

### 1. Sign In

- Go to your admin panel at `yourwebsite.com/admin`
- Use these credentials:
  - **Email**: <admin@example.com>
  - **Password**: password123
- **Important**: Change your password after first login!

### 2. Connect Your Booking Sites (Airbnb/VRBO)

1. Go to the "Airbnb/VRBO" tab
2. Click "Add Feed"
3. Enter a name (like "My Airbnb Listing")
4. Choose the platform (Airbnb or VRBO)
5. Paste the iCal URL from your listing
6. Click "Add Feed"

### 3. Set Your Pricing

1. Go to the "Rates" tab
2. Your base rate is already set
3. Add special rates for holidays or peak seasons if needed

### 4. Update Your Website

1. Go to the "Website" tab
2. Update photos and descriptions
3. Make it look beautiful for your guests!
